Skyline Tower

Commercial

A 30-story commercial building with state-of-the-art facilities and sustainable design.

Skyline Tower

Downtown, Building City Completed: January 2022 Commercial

Skyline Tower is a 30-story commercial building located in the heart of downtown. This landmark project features:

  • 500,000 square feet of Class A office space
  • LEED Platinum certification for sustainable design
  • Smart building technology for energy efficiency
  • Floor-to-ceiling windows providing panoramic city views
  • State-of-the-art conference facilities and amenities
  • Underground parking for 300 vehicles

The project was completed in January 2022 after a 24-month construction period. The building now serves as the headquarters for several major corporations and has become an iconic addition to the city skyline.

"BuildMaster exceeded our expectations with the Skyline Tower project. Their attention to detail, commitment to sustainability, and ability to deliver on time and within budget made them the ideal partner for this landmark development."
- Michael Chen, Development Director, Urban Properties

Highway Extension

Infrastructure

A major highway extension project improving connectivity and reducing traffic congestion.

Highway Extension

North County, Building City Completed: March 2023 Infrastructure

The Highway Extension project was a major infrastructure development aimed at improving connectivity and reducing traffic congestion. The project included:

  • 15-mile extension of the existing highway
  • Construction of three new interchanges
  • Two major bridge structures spanning local waterways
  • Implementation of smart traffic management systems
  • Environmental mitigation measures to protect local ecosystems
  • Noise reduction barriers in residential areas

Completed in March 2023, this project has significantly improved traffic flow and reduced commute times for thousands of daily commuters. The project was delivered on schedule despite challenging weather conditions and complex engineering requirements.

"BuildMaster's expertise in infrastructure development was evident throughout this complex project. Their ability to coordinate with multiple stakeholders and overcome engineering challenges resulted in a highway extension that has transformed our region's transportation network."
- Robert Thompson, County Transportation Director

Shopping Mall Renovation

Commercial, Renovation

Complete renovation and modernization of a 25-year-old shopping mall.

Shopping Mall Renovation

Eastside, Building City Completed: November 2022 Commercial, Renovation

This project involved the complete renovation and modernization of a 25-year-old shopping mall. The scope of work included:

  • Structural upgrades to meet current building codes
  • Complete redesign of interior spaces and common areas
  • Installation of energy-efficient lighting and HVAC systems
  • Creation of a modern food court and entertainment zone
  • Facade improvements and exterior landscaping
  • Integration of digital technology for enhanced shopping experience

The renovation was completed in November 2022 while keeping portions of the mall operational throughout the construction process. The revitalized mall has seen a 40% increase in foot traffic and has attracted several premium retail tenants.

"The transformation of our aging mall into a modern shopping destination has exceeded our expectations. BuildMaster's ability to execute this complex renovation while minimizing disruption to existing tenants was impressive. The result has revitalized our property and the surrounding community."
- Jennifer Williams, Property Manager, Eastside Mall

Residential Community

Westside, Building City Completed: August 2022 Residential

Hospital Expansion

Commercial

A major expansion of the regional medical center with state-of-the-art facilities.

Hospital Expansion

Central District, Building City Completed: April 2023 Commercial

This project involved a major expansion of the regional medical center to enhance healthcare services and capacity. The expansion included:

  • New 120,000 square foot patient tower with 100 private rooms
  • State-of-the-art surgical suite with 8 operating rooms
  • Expanded emergency department with trauma capabilities
  • Advanced diagnostic imaging center
  • Healing gardens and patient-centered design elements
  • Energy-efficient systems and sustainable building practices

Completed in April 2023, this expansion was executed while maintaining full operations of the existing hospital. The project required careful planning and phasing to minimize disruption to patient care and hospital operations.

"The complexity of expanding an operational hospital required a construction partner with exceptional planning and execution capabilities. BuildMaster delivered on all fronts, creating a state-of-the-art healthcare facility that will serve our community for generations to come."
- Dr. Elizabeth Carter, Hospital CEO
