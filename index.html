Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of construction projects do you handle?

We handle a wide range of construction projects including residential homes, commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and infrastructure projects like roads and bridges. Our team has expertise in new construction, renovations, and expansions.

How long does a typical construction project take?

The timeline for construction projects varies depending on the scope, complexity, and size. A small renovation might take a few weeks, while a large commercial building could take 12-18 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your project.

Do you provide warranties for your construction work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. We offer a 1-year warranty on all workmanship and pass through manufacturer warranties on materials and equipment. Extended warranties are also available for certain projects.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, BuildMaster Construction is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain comprehensive general liability insurance, workers' compensation, and all necessary local and state licenses. We're happy to provide documentation upon request.
